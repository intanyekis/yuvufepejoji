Martin Truex Jr. and Sherry Pollex are a super couple who meet all challenges head-on. From the race track to fighting cancer, their strength and determination have not sloped down.

NASCAR driver Martin Truex Jr. and his longtime partner, Sherry Pollex, have decided to end their relationship.

Sherry, who is 44 years old, has been fighting ovarian cancer since 2014.

Martin Truex Jr. announced his breakup in a post on Instagram. He said, “To all my fans and partners, I want to let you know that Sherry and I have made the decision to end our relationship.”

He added, “Even though we are not together anymore, I will still be there to support Sherry. We would appreciate it if you could respect our privacy and not ask any more questions about this.”

Martin Truex Jr and Sherry Pollex’s relationship has positively impacted their lives and the racing community.

Martin and Sherry’s relationship journey

Martin Truex Jr. and Sherry Pollex had a long and meaningful relationship. They have been together for many years and have supported each other through thick and thin.

The former couple faced many challenges together and built a strong bond.

Their love story began years ago when they first met. They quickly formed a deep connection and became inseparable.

Pollex’s ovarian cancer journey

Back in 2014, Pollex got some terrible news. The doctors said she had Stage 3 ovarian cancer.

They told her there was only a 30% chance she would make it through the next five years.

But guess what? She’s been fighting cancer for eight years now. It goes away for a while, but sooner or later, it comes back.

In July 2022, Pollex told a reporter named Jeff Gluck that her cancer had returned. This time, it spread to her lungs.

The doctors tried different treatments, but none of them worked very well.

She is still fighting cancer with utmost determination and will hopefully recover from it completely.

Hannah Brown Gushes Over Her Boyfriend Adam In New YouTube Video

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Former bachelorette Hannah Brown is head over heels for her boyfriend!

After making her relationship with Adam Woolard Instagram official, Hannah Brown shared her date night adventure with her followers on YouTube. The reality television star didn't find love during her time on The Bachelorette, but after getting back into the dating game, Hannah has finally found her guy.

Hannah Was A Frazzled Mess On Her First Date With Adam

The DWTS winner has been very private about her relationship, and didn't introduce her boyfriend to the world until Valentine's Day arrived. In her new YouTube video, Hannah talked all about Adam as she surprised him on their date night, and set the table with his favourite things.

While deciding her outfit and makeup for the big night, Hannah said she'd only put on a little makeup because Adam thought she was beautiful the way she is.

Hannah had been taking notes during their dates, and prepared ahead of time to bring him his favorite wine and red velvet cake. "I'll get some brownie points for that," she joked.

Admitting the reason she had been private about her relationship, Hannah said it was because people were "mean" and she wanted to protect Adam. "It's hard for me to talk about it," she said, promising she'd try and open up about her relationship in the future.

"I have had it all to myself until other people let the cat out of the bag," she added, referring to the time paparazzi in Los Angeles snapped her running errands with Adam.

"I'm very happy and that's really cool. I like him, he's a rock, a shiny one."

Hannah shared with her followers that she played it cool on their first date and didn't put a lot of thought into it. "I was a frazzled mess," she said, announcing that she turned up for the date in a sweatshirt, and even rode her bike there.

"I didn't know how hot he was until I got there."

Hannah also mentioned that she was nervous, and pronounced everything wrong at the restaurant, but "he thought it was really cute" and "didn't find me dumb or annoying".

Although Adam isn't seen on camera, we see him arrive at the end of the video, with two bouquets of flowers in his arms. Clearly, Hannah has found a keeper.

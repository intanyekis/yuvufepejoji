Rick Ross Early Life and Education

Rick Ross was born on January 28, 1976, in Clarksdale, Mississippi, as William Leonard Roberts II. He was raised in Carol City, Florida, and went to Miami Carol City Senior High School.

He was interested in football and was awarded a scholarship to Albany State University in Georgia, a historically black college. He dropped out after a year to pursue his musical love.

Rick Ross Music Career

Rick Ross began his career as Teflon Da Don in the mid-1990s. He joined 8Ball & MJG’s Suave House Records. Later, he joined Def Jam-distributed Slip-n-Slide Records. He adopted the stage name Rick Ross after legendary drug boss “Freeway” Rick Ross.

His 2006 debut single “Hustlin’” was a hit and landed him a multi-million dollar deal with Def Jam. In the same year, his debut album “Port of Miami” topped the Billboard 200. “Trilla” (2008), “Deeper Than Rap” (2009), “Teflon Don” (2010), “God Forgives, I Don’t” (2012), “Mastermind” (2014), “Hood Billionaire” (2014), “Black Market” (2015), “Rather You Than Me” (2017), and “Port of Miami 2” (2019) sustained his success. Critics and fans praised all of his Billboard 200 top-10 albums.

Rick Ross is noted for his husky voice and passionate songs about his opulent lifestyle, criminal exploits, and Miami society. He has worked with Jay-Z, Drake, Lil Wayne, Kanye West, DJ Khaled, John Legend, Nicki Minaj, Chris Brown, Future, Nas, and others. He has received four Grammy nominations.

Rick Ross Maybach Music Group

Rick Ross established his own record label, Maybach Music Group (MMG), in 2009, which is distributed by Warner Bros. Records. He signed Meek Mill, Wale, French Montana, Gunplay, Omarion, Stalley, Rockie Fresh, Fat Trel, Tracy T, and more brilliant artists to his label.

Under the MMG moniker, he also published many compilation albums, including “Self Made Vol. 1” (2011), “Self Made Vol. 2” (2012), “Self Made Vol. 3” (2013), and “MMG Priorities Vol. 5” (2016). MMG has become one of the most successful and well-known rap labels.

Rick Ross Business Ventures

Rick Ross is an entrepreneur who has engaged in a variety of business projects in addition to his music career. He likes Wingstop, a fast-food restaurant franchise specializing in chicken wings. Under his company, Boss Wings Enterprises LLC, he owns over 25 Wingstop locations across the country.

He also owns multiple Checkers and Rally franchises, another fast-food restaurant business that provides burgers, fries, hot dogs, and other items. He has also been a spokesperson for various businesses and goods, including Ciroc vodka, Reebok footwear, Belaire Rose champagne, Luc Belaire sparkling wine, mCig electronic cigarettes, and Rich hair care products.

Tom Fridley Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Tom Fridley (born February 15, 1965) is an American film and television actor.The son of Ellen Travolta, stepson of Jack Bannon, nephew of John Travolta and Joey Travolta, cousin of Jonathan Rau, and half-brother of Molly Allen, Fridley has played supporting roles in television and film.His film credits include The Karate Kid with Ralph Macchio and Pat Morita, Max Dugan Returns with Matthew Broderick and Jason Robards, Friday the 13th Part VI: Jason Lives, Summer Camp Nightmare and Moving Target with Jason Bateman and Jack Wagner. Television credits include guest starring roles on the hit television programs 21 Jump Street opposite Johnny Depp and Richard Grieco, Fantasy Island opposite Ricardo Montalban; and Our House opposite Wilford Brimley and Shannen Doherty. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿

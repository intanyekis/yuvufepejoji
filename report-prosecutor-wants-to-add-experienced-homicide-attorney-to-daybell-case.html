Report: Prosecutor wants to add experienced homicide attorney to Daybell case

Publish date: 2024-09-06

REXBURG, Idaho (CBS2) — Madison County prosecuting attorney Rob Wood wants to add a veteran homicide and death penalty attorney to his team, EastIdahoNews.com reports.

The request to add Rachel Smith, an attorney from Missouri to the Chad and Lori Daybell case, will be reviewed on Monday.

So far, no one has been charged with Tylee and JJ’s deaths, but the case is still an active investigation.

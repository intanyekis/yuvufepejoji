Fact-Check: No, Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell Are not Dating

Publish date: 2024-05-31

The online rumor mill is spinning stories of romance between Hollywood’s Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell. However, jumping further into the glitz and gossip uncovers a different reality.

Let’s sort through the rumors about this alleged celebrity romance. Examining reliable sources and the duo’s statements, we’ll unravel whether this hot headline is true or simply one more fiction in the hurricane of celebrity rumors.

The Claim

Sydney Sweeney, the prominent American actress is dating her co-star Glen Powell from ‘Anyone but You.’

The Ruling

FALSE

The claim that Sydney Sweeney is dating Glen Powell is False as indicated by multiple reputable and reliable sources moreover by Sydney as well. 

Fact

‘Anyone But You’ co-stars Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell sparked dating rumors earlier in recent months. Their chemistry has been hot, however, the rumors were hard on the actor, as confirmed by Sydney.

The actress addressed the rumors that she has kept her relationship with Jonathan Davino private, and that’s why people are shipping Sydney and Glen together. 

Evidence

Official Confirmation: It is officially confirmed by actress Sydney that the rumor of their relationship is false. Both of them had denied any romantic involvement many times.

No Credible Supporting Sources: There are no credible sources that could support the claim.

Sweeney’s Engagement: Sydney Sweeney has officially accepted that she has kept her relationship private with Jonathan Davino.

Media Outlets: Multiple reputable and reliable media outlets such as Page Six, People and Los Angeles Times have debunked the potential relationship rumors.                          

Explanation

Recently, Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell sparked dating rumors because of their hot chemistry. However, upon thorough investigation, we found that the rumors were bogus and the ‘Anyone But You’ co-stars were not dating. 

“Rumors hit him hard”- Sydney

Sydney Sweeney addressed the rumors by saying that the widespread rumors had impacted him so hard and she was not happy to see him like that. She further added that she cares for him so much and cannot see him in that situation. 

The duo had a great experience together and they cherish every moment shared. They enjoy each other’s company and are good friends.

Sydney’s relationship

While addressing the rumors, Sydney Sweeney made public that she has kept her relationship with Jonathan Davino, the producer, private due to which fans shipped her with Glen Powell, as they were not aware of her relationship with Davino.   

She mentioned to the outlet that everyone was interminably inquisitive about her relationships and their nature. She emphasized the significance of having something personal only for herself.

She further expressed that she had been in a steady relationship for an extended period, highlighting how being in a steady relationship is abnormal within the industry and for her age. She reflected on the phenomenon of celebrity, pointing out that people frequently elevate individuals before dismantling them.

The Internet is filled with misinformation regarding Sydney’s relationships and her romantic involvement with Glen Powell. However, the actress has debunked these rumors very clearly. 

Conclusion

In the symphony of celebrity gossip, the alleged romance between Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell plays a discordant note.

Despite online whispers and chemistry on-screen, Sydney Sweeney unequivocally clarified that the relationship rumors were misleading.

Sweeney reclaims her personal space amid the hurricane of celebrity speculation with a heartfelt explanation about her private relationship with Jonathan Davino and reflections on the industry’s scrutiny.

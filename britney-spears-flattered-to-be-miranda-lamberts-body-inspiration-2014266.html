Publish date: 2024-08-04

Miranda Lambert is all about the “Work B–ch.” Britney Spears expressed her love and support for the country megastar via Instagram on Wednesday, June 25, after Lambert recently cited the “Toxic” singer as her motivation.

Blake Shelton‘s wife, 30, looking slimmer and prettier than ever, told Women’s Health in its June 2014 issue that she was inspired by Spears’ toned bod. “Like if I put on Britney, I can picture Britney’s body and I’m like, ‘Oh! Faster!'” Lambert said.

The pop princess, 32, discovered Lambert’s sweet statement through her younger sister, country singer Jamie Lynn Spears. Sharing a split of Lambert’s Women’s Health cover and her accompanying quote, Spears captioned the pic: “So flattered by this @mirandalambert! My sister @jamielynnspears just showed me. You look amazing!!”

Lambert, responding to Spears’ message, wrote: “This is so cool! Thanks girls!”

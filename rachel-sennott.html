    Rachel Sennott Facts

  • Rachel is a dead ringer for actress Emmy Rossum, who is famous for starring as Fiona Gallagher in the American television series, Shameless.
  • She received the ‘Rising Star’ award at the Philadelphia Jewish Film Festival in November 2020.
  • She aspires to direct a feature film someday.
  • Rachel was named in the ‘Six Best Comedians of the Alt Scene’ lists by both Time Out New York and Pop Dust magazines in 2019.
  • She accidentally made her foray into comedy after visiting an open mic night while on a date during her freshman year in college.
  • Her short film Shiva Baby (2018) was nominated in the ‘Best Canadian Feature Film’ category at the Toronto International Film Festival. Moreover, this film was also one of the contenders in the ‘Best Narrative Feature’ category at South by Southwest.
  • Rachel shares her birthdate with several prominent celebrities such as Jeremy Irons, Trisha Yearwood, Rosemary Harris, Jimmy Fallon, Alison Sweeney, Twiggy Lawson, Danielle Panabaker, Katrina Bowden, Soledad O’Brien, Michael Symon, Bill Medley, and several others.

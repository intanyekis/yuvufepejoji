Amanda Wilcox Patterson, better known by her stage name Amanda Noelle, is a well-known Christian singer and worship leader. The vocalist is most known for her work in Christian pop and contemporary praise music.

Amanda was also a member of the Kimber Rising band with her sister before going solo in 2013 and releasing Beautiful Name. With her singing and songwriting abilities, she has captivated the hearts of millions of people.

Is Amanda Noelle Married? Know Her Relationship

As of 2021, Amanda Noelle is a happily married woman. Her husband is none other than Reid Patterson. The duo started dating in 2015.

Later, Reid proposed to Amanda on March 23, 2015, for marriage, and the lovebirds tied the knot in November 2015 in the presence of their close ones. Currently, the pair lives a joyful life in Franklin, Tennessee, where Kenda Benward also resides with her family.

Amanda Noelle Has a Kid With Her Partner

The husband and wife duo Amanda and Reid are proud parents of a daughter. The duo welcomed a daughter, Adalyn Rae Patterson, as their first child on March 22, 2017.

The Love is Greater singer keeps posting pictures with her daughter and husband on her Instagram and Facebook accounts. Moreover, she travels to new places with her family whenever she gets time. Besides this, the mother of one is also an animal lover and has a pet dog, Lillian Bear Patterson.

Amanda Noelle is a Cancer Survivor

Amanda Noelle was diagnosed with thyroid and colon cancer in 2014. The doctor told her that he detected a giant tumor after few check-ups but later confirmed that she had stage 4 cancer.

Noelle's family and friends were constantly at her side during her tough time. After numerous chemotherapies, surgeries, and countless medical visits, she was confirmed cancer-free in 2015.

Since then, the mother of a daughter has maintained a healthy lifestyle. Furthermore, Noelle is assisting others who are going through similar difficulties of cancer.

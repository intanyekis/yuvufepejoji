Gucci Mane Drops $250K Bands On New Diamond Teeth

Publish date: 2024-06-27

Gucci Mane has added some new ice to his veneers, but it’s very different than the previous grills he used to rock.

Over the 4th of July weekend, La Flare shared photos of his diamond-studded teeth on Instagram, making sure to note the expensive price tag that came with it. He had jewelry to match, too.

“A quarter Milly in GuWop grilly,” he wrote. “250k in my smile.”

The big purchase comes on the heels of the release of his new compilation album, So Icy Summer. The 24-track project was released on Friday (July 3) and features appearances from the likes of 21 Savage, Future, Moneybagg Yo, Young Thug and Lil Baby, along with artists from Gucci’s 1017 imprint.

Ahead of the release, Gucci also apologized to his label home Atlantic Records after he called them “polite racists” on Twitter and announced he was looking to go independent.

“I’m not going independent but so respect those who go that route,” he tweeted on July 1. “I apologize for my rude harsh language lately I’m a do better #1017/Atlantic.”

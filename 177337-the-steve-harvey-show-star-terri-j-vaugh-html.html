Terri J Vaughn of 'The Steve Harvey Show' Stuns in White Two-Piece in Photos on Her 50th Birthday

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Terri J. Vaugh celebrates her birthday with new photos, which she shares with her excited fans on social media.

Terri J. Vaughn first became famous after she appeared in the popular sitcom "The Steve Harvey Show" as high school secretary "Lovita Alizé Jenkins-Robinson."

Although she has featured in and directed other movies, Terri admits that the show gave her, her big break during an interview with Atlanta Black Star.

The "Meet the Browns" star recently turned fifty, and to celebrate, she went for a photoshoot that showed off her many sides. To the delight of her fans, Terri shared some up-close photos from the shoot via Instagram.

The first series of snapshots shows the director's sultry side as she poses by a window in a white lingerie set and later on, a white shirt.

In the next series of shots, the 50-year-old shared a behind-the-scenes video that shows her ruffling her new short hair.

Writing a lengthy caption, the director stated that:

"I've starred on a few TV series, I've produced many movies, I've directed several movies. I've been married, divorced, and married again. I've given birth to 3 fantastic healthy children. I've awakened every day to be the best I can be. And it's all led me to this day, and I have so much more to do!"

Fans didn't hesitate in congratulating the star and wishing her well, especially as in her marriage.

Back in 2008, PEOPLE exclusively disclosed that Terri and her husband, Karon Riley, eloped in Las Vegas. The report also revealed that the pair were expecting their first child together.

Four years later, Essence also exclusively shared that the actress that love is 'better the second time around.'

The actress, who was previously married to Derrick Carolina, explained that her second marriage made her more aware of her shortcomings as a person.

Additionally, the sexual aspect of her relationship is better. Speaking on romance, Terri divulged that she and her significant other make a conscious effort to work at their marriage. In her words:

"Equal responsibility for the romance through notes, e-mails, phone calls, hugs, holding hands, and scheduled date nights."

The duo’s plan seems to be working as they look loved-up and worry-free in the photo she shared recently

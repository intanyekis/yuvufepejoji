Jazwares to Produce Ring of Honor Action Figures? (Video)

Publish date: 2024-07-25

During most AEW Fan Fest events toy company Jazwares reveals upcoming AEW action figures to collectors, whether it be physical prototypes, early digital renders or even just teaser images of the talent coming down the line.

During Double or Nothing Fan Fest this past weekend, Jazwares had a video play during their presentation, a hack from Danhausen, that seemed to tease Ring of Honor action figures in the works.

Such talent they showed included:

  • Claudio Castagnoli
  • Bryan Danielson
  • The Young Bucks
  • Danhausen
  • Adam Cole
  • The Briscoes
  • and many more!

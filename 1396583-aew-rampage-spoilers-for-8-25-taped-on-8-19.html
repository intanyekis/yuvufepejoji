AEW Rampage Spoilers For 8/25 (Taped On 8/19)

Publish date: 2024-08-18

On August 19, All Elite Wrestling taped matches for the August 25 episode of AEW Rampage from the Rupp Arena in Lexington, Kentucky.

The spoilers, courtesy of PWInsider, are as follows:

  • AAA Latin American Championship: QT Marshall (c) defeated Gravity
  • AEW International Championship: Orange Cassidy (c) defeated Aaron Solo
  • Serpentico defeated Truth Magnum
  • Luchasaurus defeated local talent
  • Toni Storm & Saraya defeated Hikaru Shida & Britt Baker

WrestleZone will have live coverage of AEW Rampage as it airs on August 25.

York County Murder – Suicide: Steve Bowersox and Wife Brenda Bowersox Both Killed in Murder-Suicide at Home

York County Murder – Suicide: The married couple who appeared to have committed a murder-suicide over the weekend has been identified by the York County Coroner. Authorities responded to a complaint of gunshots on Glen Hollow Drive in Springettsbury Township, where the sad occurrence occurred.

They arrived at the scene at 8:56 a.m. on Sunday, July 23, 2023, and found Brenda Bowersox, 55, dead. She sadly died at the scene at 9:17 a.m. after suffering multiple gunshot wounds.

In the same home, 60-year-old Steve Bowersox was discovered with significant gunshot wounds that he had self-inflicted. He was quickly transported to WellSpan York Hospital for medical care, but at 10:31 a.m., he was declared dead.

The York County Coroner determined that Steve Bowersox shot his wife before turning the gun on himself, classifying the occurrence as a murder-suicide.

Authorities are looking into the circumstances leading up to this unfortunate incident. Standard toxicology tests have been performed as part of the investigation; nevertheless, there won’t be any autopsies.

Originating from Stockton California, Nick Diaz is a well known mixed martial artist who currently competes in the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). As of 2012, Nick Diaz’ net worth is estimated to be around $800 thousand.

Prior to his career at UFC, Diaz has fought in several MMA Promotions like Elite Xtreme Combat, and Pride Fighting Championships. He was once hailed a WEC, IEC and Strikeforce Welterweight champion.

According to reports, Nick Diaz passion for learning martial arts started on his teenage years because he was bullied by some teenagers. His career as a professional mixed martial artist started in 2001, right after he turned 18. His first victory followed shortly after he defeated Mike Wick.

Nick Diaz is known for his impressive endurance, trash talking and hurling insults to his opponents before and during the fight. He is also an accomplished Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belter.

His younger brother Nate Diaz also competes in the UFC.

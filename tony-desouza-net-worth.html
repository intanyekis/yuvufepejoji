Tony DeSouza is a Peruvian professional mixed martial artist with an estimated net worth of $10 thousand.

Tony DeSouza has been wrestling throughout his high school and college years in Los Angeles. He learned the techniques of Brazilian jiu jitsu thru the expertise of John Lewis and Andre Pederneiras.

In 2000, he played at CFF-The Cobra Classic and won via submission to Ben Melendez. DeSouza appeared during the UFC 31 match against Steve Berger and won via unanimous decision in May 2001. He appeared on The Ultimate Fighter 5 and served as wrestling coach for Team Penn.

Tony DeSouza was born on July 26, 1974 in Lima, Peru.

The Cast Of All American, Ranked By Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-06-27

The fifth season of All American is finally upon us. The hit CW series concluded its fourth season in May, with a total of 20 episodes airing. Barely five months later, fans can begin diving into the next chapter of the April Blair show.

The confirmation of the return has been as thrilling for audiences as it has been for the All American cast, who have often been adulatory when speaking about the show. The mostly African-American cast comprises Daniel Ezra, Taye Diggs and Calesha “Bre-Z” Murray, among others.

Diggs agreed to join the show because he felt that it resonated with a lot of current, real-life issues that especially affect the African-American community. The Private Practice star is one of the more experienced names in the cast, with a good number of his co-stars enjoying their breakout roles on the show.

Other cast members include Samantha Logan, Greta Onieogou, Michael Evans Behling and Cody Christian. Behling’s salary from the series has helped to enhance his net worth, but does he make it on the list of the richest actors from All American?

10 Chelsea Tavares - $1.5 Million

Michael Evans Behling is said to have a net worth of $1 million, which falls short of the ten richest people from the cast of All American. Chelsea Tavares just about makes the list, however, with her $1.5 million net worth.

Tavares plays Patience Robinson, a main character on the show starting Season 3.

9 Daniel Ezra - $1.5 Million

Daniel Ezra is also currently valued at $1.5 million. The British actor plays the leading role in All American, as a talented football player known as Spencer James.

Ezra’s character was inspired by the real-life, former NFL star, Spencer Paysinger. His other credits include the BBC productions The Missing and Undercover.

8 Monét Mazur - $2 Million

Monét Mazur has been acting since the early ‘90s. For all her work, she has managed to amass a net worth of $ 2 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

In All American, Mazur features as Laura Fine-Baker, a lawyer who is married to Taye Diggs’ character, Billy Baker.

7 Karimah Westbrook - $2 Million

Like Monét Mazur, Karimah Westbrook is also said to be worth around $2 million. She portrays Grace James in All American, Spencer’s mom.

One of the early conflicts on the show also involves an old fling between her and Billy, which raises questions about the identity of Spencer’s father.

6 Samantha Logan - $2 Million

Samantha Logan is another whose net worth is estimated to be around $2 million. The 25-year-old appears in All American as Olivia Baker, daughter of Billy and Laura Fine-Baker.

Logan is also known for her work in Teen Wolf, The Fosters and 13 Reasons Why on Netflix.

5 Greta Onieogou - $3 Million

Greta Onieogou’s character in All American is Layla Keating, Spencer’s high school ex-girlfriend. Layla also grows into a music record producer as the show progresses.

Celebrity Net Worth estimates that Onieogou’s assets are valued at $3 million today.

4 Bre-Z - $4 Million

Calesha “Bre-Z” Murray is fast becoming a very familiar face on the small screen, having also starred as Freda Gatz in Empire. Her All American character is Tamia “Coop” Cooper, Spencer’s close friend and confidante.

Bre-Z is believed to be worth around $4 million in 2022.

3 Dina Meyer - $5 Million

Dina Meyer is one of the few names on this list who are not a part of the very main cast of All American. Her character, Gwen Adams is first introduced in Season 2, and carries on in a guest capacity through Seasons 3 and 4.

Nonetheless, Meyer is one of the richer cast members on the show, with a net worth of $5 million.

2 Casper Van Dien - $7 Million

Like Dina Meyer, Casper Van Dien only appears in All American in a guest role, starting Season 1 until Season 3. He plays Harold Adams, Gwen’s ex-husband with whom he has a son called Asher Adams (Cody Christian).

Van Dien is the second-wealthiest actor on this list, with a net worth of $7 million.

1 Taye Diggs - $14 Million

Given his extensive experience, it is not surprising that Taye Diggs is the richest cast member of All American. Through his stints in Private Practice, Murder in the First and The Best Man movies, he has managed to accrue a net worth of around $14 million.

Diggs’ Billy Baker is a basketball coach, who recruits Spencer James for his Beverly Hills High football team.

Taylor Swift Once Had Sir Ian McKellen Kicked Out Of His Apartment

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift expanded her real estate portfolio by purchasing a neighboring townhome, creating a penthouse with 10 bedrooms and 10 bathrooms.
  • Sir Ian McKellen was evicted from Peter Jackson's apartment when Swift bought it, but he had no hard feelings and even became friends with her.
  • Swift offered her penthouse to friend Sophie Turner during her divorce from Joe Jonas, showing her generosity and support.

Taylor Swift has quite a real estate portfolio. Investment in properties is just part of how Swift adds to her current net worth. With homes ranging from the East to the West Coast and everywhere in between, Swift can change her locale at the drop of a hat and have a change of scenery that fits her mood.

While some of the properties that Swift owns are away from the hustle and bustle of the city, others are located directly in the heart of New York City. As such, when Swift decides to stay in more metropolitan areas, the paparazzi keep track of her every move. But, spending time in the city also allows Swift to see if there are any new properties that catch her eye. It happened in 2015 when Swift was made aware of the fact that a townhouse next to hers was for sale.

Wanting to make a huge penthouse out of her existing townhome and the one next door, Swift purchased the property. However, in doing so, Swift got Sir Ian McKellen kicked out of his apartment.

Sir Ian McKellen Was Staying In Peter Jackson's Apartment

McKellen's rental home was up for sale while he lived there

In 2015, McKellen was staying in Peter Jackson's apartment in New York City. Peter Jackson was away at the time and given that McKellen did not own any property in New York City, calling London home instead, Jackson offered his townhome to McKellen while he was in town.

McKellen accepted the offer and settled into Jackson's townhome.

In June 2015, McKellen was in New York City to be co-Grand Marshall of the New York Pride parade. The parade took place just two days after the Supreme Court ruled that same-sex marriage was a right of all Americans. As such, there was a lot to celebrate.

McKellen was in town to promote his film, Mr. Holmes. He was also in attendance for other professional functions and to accept awards as well. As such, it made sense for McKellen to use Jackson's townhome if it was going to be vacant given that his time in New York was going to be lengthy.

However, while McKellen was staying in the townhome, Jackson had let him know that it was on the market. Given the finicky nature of the New York City real estate market, it was not certain how quickly the townhome would sell.

When Jackson's neighbor caught wind that his abode was for sale, she jumped on the opportunity. That neighbor was Swift.

Taylor Swift Purchased Peter Jackson's Apartment After It Went On The Market

Taylor bought a neighboring townhome to expand her property

Swift loved the location of her townhome so much that when the opportunity to purchase Jackson's townhome next door became available, Swift purchased it. This meant that Swift's square footage of her townhome was about to dramatically increase. As soon as the walls were dropped, Taylor's townhome became a penthouse.

The TriBeCa real estate was converted to become a penthouse with "10 bedrooms and 10 bathrooms, a billiards room, and an incredible sweeping staircase." This allowed Swift to have her peace and quiet while in the city given the space of her home.

This proved to not be quite large enough for the Lavender Haze singer, as, in 2018, Swift also purchased yet another townhouse in the building — property is another way Swift spends her massive fortune.

The new townhome was located directly below Swift. There have not been any details to date about whether this will be a standalone property or one that will also be worked into the existing floor plan of the penthouse.

Given that she is currently on tour and not staying in New York, Swift has offered the penthouse in TriBeCa to her friend, Sophie Turner while she navigates her divorce from Joe Jonas.

Unlike McKellen, there is no fear of Turner being evicted from the home before she is ready to go. But even when he was evicted, McKellen had a good sense of humor about the situation.

Sir Ian McKellen Has No Hard Feelings Against Taylor Swift

McKellen joked about Taylor Swift kicking him out of his rental

After he was given his eviction notice, McKellen had a short time to find a new place to stay for his remaining time in New York City. McKellen did not harbor any ill feelings against Swift, however. Instead, he found the situation amusing and even became friends with Swift in the process.

"When I was living in Peter Jackson’s apartment in New York, Taylor Swift bought it while I was there and I was thrown out before I wanted to leave," McKellen explained. "That hardly puts me in Taylor Swift’s team, does it?"

McKellen went on to say, "She bought [the apartment], she had every right to – I was just lodging there for free! She did ask me to appear with Patrick Stewart at her show in LA, but I had something else to do that night."

Stewart and McKellen even went on to try to join Swift's Squad as a result of the situation, tweeting out, "So @IanMcKellen recited Bad Blood and I did Blank Space on @NPRAskMeAnother. May we join the squad, @taylorswift13?"

Swift replied to the two saying, "Thanks for reciting my lyrics, @IanMcKellen and @SirPatStew! You've made my day. You two are ULTIMATE Squad Goals." Even Taylor Swift recognizes that Stewart and McKellen are friendship goals.

While McKellen did get kicked out of the plush pad he was staying in, compliments of Jackson, he had no hard feelings towards Swift when she bought the townhome. After all, it was a temporary fix and McKellen got to enjoy his time in New York City, leaving as a Swiftie before it was all said and done.

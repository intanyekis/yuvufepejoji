Pattie Mallette Says Baby Justin Biebers First Word Was Money

Publish date: 2024-06-05

No 'mama' or 'dada' would do for The Biebs' first words! His mom, Pattie, revealed that he was thinking about the dollar signs before he could even walk -- and his very first word was 'money!' Justin Bieber's mom, Pattie Mallette revealed during a completely candid "Ask Pattie" Twitter session on Dec. 8, that Justin's very first word was "money," and he would even subsitute it out for "mommy!" This fun throwback memory got Pattie reminiscing about the adorable baby Justin was!

Justin Bieber’s First Word Was Money: Mom Pattie Mallette Recalls Childhood

Justin, 19, has such an adorable relationship with his mom, and the two have clearly had their close bond since the very beginning. Pattie actually couldn’t help but laugh when a fan asked what the first word Justin said was — because he had such a funny one!

“Money! Hahahaha! Instead of Mommy he said Money & Daddy!” Pattie said on Twitter.

Another fan asked Pattie about her favorite moments with Justin from his childhood, and she had the sweetest answer. “Bedtimes. I would tuck him in, read to him, pray with him & talk & giggle sometimes for an hour or more,” she said wistfully. “I miss that!” she sighed.

Pattie Mallette Reacts To Justin Bieber’s Future Kids

Pattie was temporarily thrown for a loop when another inquiring fan asked Pattie what she thought about Justin becoming a parent himself one day. “Ahhh omg could u imagine me a Grandma already!?” Pattie answered. “Hopefully that’s a long way in the future!”

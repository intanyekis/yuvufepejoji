Ciara Hanna is an American actress and model who is best known for portraying roles such as Gia Moran in Power Rangers Megaforce, Summer/Forrester Model in The Bold and the Beautiful, and Nicole Parker in Blood Lake: Attack of the Killer Lampreys. She has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as Jonas, The Protector, Big Time Rush, Sam & Cat, Anger Management, New Girl, Hawaii Five-0, Project X, The Unauthorized Melrose Place Story, Devil’s Revenge, and many more. Ciara has been featured in the music videos of songs like Jay Sean‘s Do You Love Me.

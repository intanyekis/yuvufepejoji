Photos Of The NBA Reporter Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-28

We LOVE to see a female sports news reporter! There’s nothing more inspiring for young women than to see a stellar female on T.V, bringing all the latest sports news to a major sports station. Take Cassidy Hubbarth, for a prime example. Born in 1984, Hubbarth hit the ground running early in her broadcasting career, when she won a Southeast Emmy for her work on SEC Gridiron Live at the young age of just 22. From there, she worked her way to the top and joined ESPN in 2010.

By 2013, Hubbarth became a full-time news anchor, hosting all kinds of major sports shows including SportsCenter, NBA tonight and many more. It’s no discussion that Cassidy obviously has a face that’s made for television, and she certainly fits in on the red carpet.

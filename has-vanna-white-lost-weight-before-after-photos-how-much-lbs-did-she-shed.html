Learn About Vanna White Weight Loss Journey: How Much LBS Did She Shed?

Then 25-year-old Vanna White was 25 lbs heavier than she is today prior to auditioning for Wheel of Fortune according to the article published.

She was aware of the issue her weight would have on her from getting the coveted game-show job. The host realized she must do something.

After a brief moment of introspection, the TV personality thought to herself that she must make some drastic change in her life.

Following that decision, she decided to initiate a healthy lifestyle with a proper diet and planned workout like most of us would start.

The 61-year-old host started running and surprisingly in a short period of time, she lost 10 pounds, stated the Wheel of Fortune host.

Motivated by such a significant change in her physical appearance she began to take a proper guided balanced diet replacing all junk foods.

She reportedly replaced all the bad foods devoid of all the nutrients and minerals essential for the body with fruits and vegetables.

Yet again, she lost the other 15 pounds just by eating clean. “Eating healthy food gave her more energy and a nicer figure,” stated the host.

After a substantial weight loss of 25 pounds in total from running and eating right, the host felt prepared to audition for the game show.

It must be her diet and exercise program that made her stand out among others, says the host now that she looks back at the audition in 1982.

Ultimately, her hard work paid off well. She grabbed the position of the sole hostess on the Wheel of Fortune on December 13, 1982.

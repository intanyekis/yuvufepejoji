Danielle Bradbery Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Who is Danielle Bradbery?

Danielle Bradbery is the Academy of Country Music Awards nominated singer. She is renowned for her country singing and has won The Voice S4.

Last, in 2009, she was in the Live Forever Tour along with the singer, Kane Brown.

Danielle Bradbery- Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Siblings

She was born Danielle Simone Bradbery on the 23rd of June 1996 in Houston, Texas. She is the daughter of Danielle Bradbery (father) and Gloria Redden Martinez (mother).

Danielle has an elder sister, Monica. They were raised in Cypress, Texas. They are of British-Mexican-Irish ethnicity.

Also, Danielle Bradbery also has three half-siblings, Drake, Drew, and Collin. She joined the Cypress Ranch High School in Cypress.

Danielle Bradbery- Professional Career

Singing Career

In 2013, Danielle Bradbery has started her musical journey as an artist with the American singing platform, The Voice. Through her magical sound, she impressed the judges Adam Levine, Blake Shelton, and Usher to turn their chairs.

For the rest of the season, she chose the mentor, Blake. On the 17th of June 2013, she won the competition making her the youngest winner of the show.

On the 19th of June 2013, Danielle Bradbery signed with the recording label, Big Machine Records. Under the recording label, she released her first song, The Heart of Dixie on the 16th of July 16. The song peaked at the position of 65 in the US Country Billboard. Also, the song earned Gold certification by RIAA.

Following the song, she launched her very first self-titled album under the recording label, Big Machine. While talking about the chart performance, the album was in the 33rd position on the US Country Billboard in the yearly chart.

On the 1st of December 2017, Danielle Bradbery dropped her 2nd album, I Don’t Believe We’ve Met that peaked at the 6th position in the US Billboard chart. Till March 2018, 21,900 copies of the album were sold in the United States.

Tours

Besides singing, she is also active in musical tours. In 2013, she met her very first musical tour that happens to be Beat This Summer Tour along with the country singer, Brad Paisley.

Also, Danielle Bradbery had been a part of the tours like 2014- We’re Not Invisible Tour, 2015- Platinum Tour, and 2018- Life Changes Tour.
